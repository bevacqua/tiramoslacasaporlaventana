When is it?

After over three years together, we're getting married!

The wedding party is on april 16th, at day time — from 12:30 pm and until 09:00 pm. There's going to be a cocktail, followed by dancing, barbecue, more dancing, drinks, desserts, cotillon, and more.

If you have kids, babies, or people you've otherwise created, you can keep calm and bring them. We have planned plenty of activities so that they have fun as well!

There's lots of room to park your car or helicopter, so don't worry about that!

We recommend 20 hours of fasting, a couple of dance lessons, and an urge to have a good time!

Where is it?

At the Álamos de Cañuelas ranch, a gorgeous countryside field packed with fresh air, chickens, goatlings, and even peacocks! Alas, we won't be hunting for food... we'll be enjoying delicious argentine barbecue, but the goatlings and peacocks roaming around the ranch won't be included!

The reception cocktail is right outside the lounge. For lunch, we'll head into the lounge, so that we don't fall prey to global warming.

The drink bar, tea service, candy buffet, chickens, and fresh air will all be found outside the lounge.

Álamos is on national route 3 — RN3. For directions click here.

We hope you like Álamos as much as we do!

What should I wear?

Our dress code is casual elegant.

When picking your dress, keep in mind that — weather permitting — the party will take place both indoors and outdoors.

To our fancy women: we suggest that you bring a purse with an extra pair of shoes!

Kids are exempt from our dress code, and they can dress as they please, so don't feel compelled to have them wear a suit, a shirt, and shoes if the kid doesn't want to!

Keep in mind you'll have the choice of dancing and eating to your heart's content, meaning that it'll be essential to dress comfortably.
